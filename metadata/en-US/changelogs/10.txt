Feudal Tactics 1.3.0

This update brings several new features and improvements. Highlights include:
- changed game rule: combining two spearmen is now possible
- the seed can now be copied and pasted easily
- new option to choose the starting position and color
- new turn indicator icon
- new spectate mode after the player is beaten
- optimized saving speed and save file size
- added XXLarge map size
- improved font readability
- easy and medium bots are now less agressive towards the player
